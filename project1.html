The War Destroyed Our Campus -
But Not Our Mission

Donate Now Subscribe
Опубліковано: July 29 2025
Поширити:

In this difficult time, when war daily tests the endurance and emotional health of the Ukrainian people, the Ukrainian Evangelical Theological Seminary (UETS) continues to be a center of hope, healing, and restoration.

On June 28, the Artkaleidoscope conference was held at the UETS — a dynamic meeting of practitioners, educators, and specialists who work with faith, united by the belief that art has the power to transform a person, helps overcome trauma, and emotionally recover.

“This campaign represents more than building walls; it’s about building a legacy of faith that will impact generations to come.”
Pastor John Smith
Senior Pastor

Key topics of the speakers

  1. “Art Therapy for Military Families — Support Through Creativity,” Victoria Ulyanova
  2. “Emotional Stability of Parents of Children with Special Needs: Techniques and Tools,” Victoria Khromets
  3. “Trust as an Inner Lantern,” Lilia Kagan
  4. “Psychotherapeutic tools for crisis work with children and adults in wartime,” Oksana Hrytsyuta
  5. “Art Therapy for Military Families — Support Through Creativity,” Victoria Ulyanova
  6. “Emotional Stability of Parents of Children with Special Needs: Techniques and Tools,” Victoria Khromets
  7. “Trust as an Inner Lantern,” Lilia Kagan
